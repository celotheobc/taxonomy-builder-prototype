HOW TO TEST (click journey)

Route A — build + resolve yourself:
  1. Click "Sales Order" to start
  2. Use + on ghost nodes: add Customer, Delivery, Delivery Item
  3. Status becomes "Needs resolution" — two coloured routes appear
  4. Click scissors on an edge (Pattern B) OR "Keep this route" (Pattern A)
  5. Status becomes Valid

Route B — shortcut (pre-loaded conflict):
  1. Switch tab to "Resolve Route Ambiguity"
  2. Graph already shows the conflict — prune or select a route

See the collapsible "How to test" panel in the app for full steps.

Note: There is no separate "add cycle" button. Ambiguity appears when
both routes to Delivery Item exist in the included graph.
